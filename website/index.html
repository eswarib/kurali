Support

Something not working? File an issue on GitHub or reach out to us at coral.voice.support@gmail.com. Before you report a bug, it helps to include:

  • Your OS and version (e.g. Ubuntu 24.04, Windows 11)
  • How you installed Coral (AppImage, .deb, MSI, etc.)
  • Coral version (from the app or release you downloaded)
  • What you expected vs what happened, and steps to reproduce
